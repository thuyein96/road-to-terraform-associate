Day 5 of Learning Terraform: Understanding Alias



When you start with Terraform, deploying to one region is easy. But as your infrastructure grows, you often need to deploy the same resources across different regions, accounts, or environments.



Instead of copying your code into multiple folders, you can use Aliases.



What is an Alias?

Think of an Alias as a nickname for your provider. It allows you to define multiple configurations for the same provider (like AWS) in a single file.
